How to Play

  1. Click Start Game to begin
  2. Watch the 3D shape carefully in the box
  3. When the shape turns green and starts spinning rapidly, click the box immediately
  4. Your reaction time will be displayed in the top-right corner
  5. Warning: Clicking too early counts as a false start!
