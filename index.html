How to Play

  1. Click Start Game to begin
  2. Watch the 3D shape carefully in the box above
  3. When the shape turns green and starts spinning rapidly, click the box immediately
  4. Your reaction time will be displayed in the top-right corner in milliseconds
  5. Warning: Clicking too early counts as a false start!
  6. Try to get the lowest reaction time possible
